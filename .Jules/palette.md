## 2024-05-23 - The "Blank Canvas" Problem
**Learning:** Users with empty inventories (new users) faced a blank screen that felt broken or confusing. "Empty States" are not just placeholders; they are critical onboarding opportunities.
**Action:** Always implement a dedicated "Empty State" component for lists that guides the user to their first action (e.g., "Add your first nutrient") rather than showing nothing.
